Game rules

  1. Both Qualifying and Race predictions can be made before the Qualifying event. lightbulb - indicates that you didn't make predictions for the event, and you can make them.
  2. Once Qualifying starts, the corresponding section of Prediction form is being locked.
  3. The Race section of Prediction form is available until the race starts.
  4. Predictions can be updated as many times as you wish while the form is available. lightbulb_outline - indicates that you've made predictions for the event, and you can still update them.
  5. Predictions for the next Grand Prix weekend can be made any time after previous Grand Prix finished.
  6. When calculating players' points for guessed places in the Qualifying, we take into account only the best lap of each driver, and do not consider anything that might happen after this lap (penalties, disqualifications and so on).
  7. When calculating players' points for guessed places in the Race, we take into account only the drivers' final places (e.g. after all referee checks has been performed and applied to the race results).
  8. So far there is no option to predict Sprint results (can be added later).

Points system

  1. Driver selection
    1. It is mandatory to fill all 5 drivers for both Qualifying and Race.
    2. For each correct driver on the top-5 list: +1 point ().
    3. For each correct driver position on the top-5 list: +2 points ().
  2. Team selection
    1. Optionally you can make one or more winning team selections (if there are any).
    2. The winning team from the selection is the one whose drivers took total places higher than drivers from rival team.
    3. Example 1: Team A drivers' places: 1st and 15th. Team B drivers' places: 7st and 8th. Team B wins.
    4. Example 2: Team A drivers' places: 2st and 13th. Team B drivers' places: 5st and 10th. It's a draw.
    5. For each correct team-winner from pair: +1 point ().
    6. For each wrong team-winner from pair: -1 point ().
    7. For each skipped selection ("vs"): 0 points.
    8. For each draw: 0 points.
  3. All player points are being summed up in order to get his total points.
  4. Some things related to points system are being or to be discussed. It is possible that some new ways of getting points will be added in the future.
